    BRANDING, LOGO & BUSINESS CARD DESIGN

    Project Background

    Prosport Management is a sports agency that represents well known golfers including Bubba Watson (who recently won
    the Masters), Aaron Badley, and Geoff Ogilvy. Our goal was to create a sophisticated look for their company that was
    flexible enough for them to expand their business to other sports.
    I developed a custom Sans Serif logotype based off of Edmondsans. I used a gray scale for their primary colors, and a
    green secondary pallet to be used on their website. I also developed brand guidelines, business cards, and letterhead for
    Prosport in time for the Masters.
